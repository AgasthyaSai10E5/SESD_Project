Top Cricket Stadiums in India

Wankhede Stadium

Located in Mumbai, Wankhede Stadium has hosted many thrilling matches, including the 2011 World Cup Final.

Eden Gardens

Eden Gardens in Kolkata is one of the oldest and most prestigious cricket stadiums in India.

M. Chinnaswamy Stadium

Located in Bangalore, this stadium is known for its lively atmosphere and great crowd support for the Indian team.

M. A. CHIDAMBARAM Stadium

Situated next the Indian Ocean, the M.A. Chidambaram Stadium will provide one of the most humid climates across all venues.

Narendra Modi Stadium

The largest sports stadium in the world will set the stage for the tournament opener.
